Our Story and Mission

XADO ISRAEL was founded with a clear vision: to provide automotive enthusiasts, DIY mechanics, and professional technicians with access to the highest quality automotive products available in the market. Our journey began with a simple observation that many vehicle owners and professionals struggled to find reliable, effective automotive care products that delivered on their promises. We set out to change that by creating an online wholesale market that combines premium product quality with exceptional customer service and competitive pricing.

Over the years, we have built strong relationships with leading manufacturers and developed deep expertise in automotive maintenance and repair solutions. This knowledge allows us to carefully curate our product selection, ensuring that every item in our catalog meets our stringent quality standards. We understand that our customers depend on us for products that work reliably and consistently, and we take that responsibility seriously. Whether you're maintaining a family vehicle or running a professional automotive service business, you can trust XADO ISRAEL to provide the solutions you need.

Our commitment extends beyond simply selling products. We view ourselves as partners in our customers' success, providing not just automotive care solutions but also the knowledge and support necessary to use them effectively. This customer-centric approach has earned us the trust of thousands of satisfied customers who return to us repeatedly for their automotive product needs. As we continue to grow, we remain dedicated to our founding principles of quality, reliability, and exceptional service.
